Rules & Guidelines

Professional standards and community guidelines that ensure a realistic and enjoyable virtual aviation experience for all Virtual BAW pilots.

Flight Operations

Professional flight standards and operational procedures

Use realistic flight planning with proper routes and altitudes
Follow standard operating procedures for all aircraft types
File proper flight plans using real-world routing when possible
Maintain professional communication on all frequencies
Complete required training before operating new aircraft types

Activity Requirements

Minimum activity standards to maintain active pilot status

Complete at least 2 flights per month to remain active
Participate in community events when available
Update your availability status if taking extended breaks
Check Discord regularly for important announcements

Community Standards

Behavioral expectations for a professional environment

Treat all members with respect and professionalism
No harassment, discrimination, or offensive behavior
Help new pilots learn and feel welcome in our community
Keep discussions aviation-related in flight channels
Report any issues to staff through appropriate channels

Communication Protocol

Professional communication standards across all platforms

Use proper aviation phraseology during flight operations
Maintain radio discipline on VATSIM/IVAO networks
Use Discord for coordination and non-operational discussions
English is the primary language for all operations

Violation Policy

Our progressive enforcement system ensures fair treatment while maintaining professional standards.

Minor Violations

Level 1

Examples:

  • Incorrect callsign usage
  • Minor SOP deviations
  • Late flight reports

Consequences:

Verbal warning and guidance provided

Moderate Violations

Level 2

Examples:

  • Repeated minor violations
  • Inappropriate communication
  • Failure to complete required training

Consequences:

Written warning and additional training required

Serious Violations

Level 3

Examples:

  • Harassment or discrimination
  • Deliberate policy violations
  • Disruptive behavior during events

Consequences:

Temporary suspension (1-4 weeks)

Severe Violations

Level 4

Examples:

  • Severe harassment or threats
  • Malicious disruption of operations
  • Violation of external network rules

Consequences:

Permanent removal from Virtual BAW
